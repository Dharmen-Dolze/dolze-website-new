Data Security

At DolzeAI Technologies Pvt Ltd, we understand that your data security is paramount. This document outlines our comprehensive approach to protecting your information, maintaining system integrity, and ensuring compliance with international security standards. We are committed to implementing industry-leading security measures to safeguard your data at every stage.

1. Security Infrastructure

Our security infrastructure is built on multiple layers of protection. We utilize enterprise-grade cloud hosting with 99.9% uptime guarantee to ensure your data is always accessible. Our multi-region data centers provide automatic failover capabilities, ensuring business continuity even during regional outages. Advanced firewalls and intrusion detection systems protect against external threats, while real-time threat monitoring and response systems enable immediate action against security incidents. We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing to identify and address potential vulnerabilities before they can be exploited.

2. Data Encryption

We implement comprehensive encryption protocols to protect your data at every stage. All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3 during transmission, ensuring secure communication between your devices and our servers. Data stored in our databases is encrypted using AES-256 encryption, providing military-grade protection for your information at rest. Our API security is enhanced with OAuth 2.0 and JWT tokens, ensuring secure authentication and authorization for all API communications. Additionally, all backups are encrypted before storage and transmission, maintaining the same level of security for your data throughout the backup process.

3. Access Controls and Authentication

We maintain strict access controls to ensure only authorized personnel can access your data. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all user accounts, adding an extra layer of security beyond traditional passwords. Our role-based access control (RBAC) system implements the principle of least privilege, ensuring users only have access to the specific resources they need to perform their tasks. We conduct regular access reviews and privilege audits to identify and remove unnecessary permissions. Session management includes automatic timeout and logout features to prevent unauthorized access from inactive sessions. Additionally, we enforce secure password policies with complexity requirements to ensure strong authentication credentials.

4. Data Processing and Storage

Your data is processed and stored with the highest security standards. All data processing occurs within secure, isolated environments that prevent unauthorized access and cross-contamination between different user datasets. We ensure that no sensitive data is stored in log files or temporary storage, minimizing the risk of data exposure. Our automatic data sanitization and secure deletion protocols ensure that data is properly cleaned and permanently removed when no longer needed. We perform regular data integrity checks and validation to maintain the accuracy and consistency of your information. Additionally, we maintain compliance with data residency requirements, ensuring your data is stored in accordance with applicable regulations and your preferences.

5. AI and Machine Learning Security

Our AI systems are designed with security in mind from the ground up. AI models are trained in isolated, secure environments that prevent unauthorized access and ensure data privacy during the training process. We maintain a strict policy that no user data is used to train third-party AI models, protecting your information from being shared with external parties. All AI interactions undergo thorough input validation and sanitization to prevent malicious inputs and ensure system integrity. Our secure model deployment process includes version control to track changes and maintain accountability. We conduct regular AI model security assessments to identify potential vulnerabilities and ensure our AI systems remain secure and trustworthy.

6. Third-Party Security

We carefully vet and monitor all third-party services to ensure they meet our high security standards. Our comprehensive vendor security assessments evaluate potential partners' security practices, infrastructure, and compliance with industry standards. We establish data processing agreements with all third-party providers that outline their security obligations and data handling requirements. Regular security audits of third-party services ensure ongoing compliance and identify any potential security gaps. All data shared with third-party services is encrypted to maintain confidentiality and prevent unauthorized access. We actively monitor third-party security incidents and maintain contingency plans to address any security issues that may arise with our partners.

7. Incident Response and Monitoring

We maintain robust incident response capabilities to quickly identify and address security threats. Our 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection systems continuously scan for potential security issues, ensuring immediate awareness of any suspicious activities. Automated alerting systems provide instant notifications when suspicious activities are detected, enabling rapid response to potential threats. Our dedicated incident response team follows defined escalation procedures to ensure appropriate actions are taken based on the severity and nature of each incident. We conduct regular security incident simulations and training exercises to keep our team prepared for real-world scenarios. During actual security incidents, we maintain transparent communication with affected users, providing timely updates and clear information about the situation and our response efforts.

8. Compliance and Certifications

We maintain compliance with international security standards to ensure your data is protected according to the highest industry requirements. Our ISO 27001 Information Security Management System certification demonstrates our commitment to implementing comprehensive security controls and best practices. SOC 2 Type II compliance validates our security controls through independent third-party audits, providing assurance that our security measures are effective and consistently applied. We maintain GDPR compliance for European users, ensuring their data rights are fully respected and protected. For California residents, we maintain CCPA compliance to uphold their privacy rights and data protection requirements. We conduct regular compliance audits and assessments to ensure ongoing adherence to these standards and identify areas for continuous improvement.

9. Data Backup and Recovery

We ensure your data is protected against loss through comprehensive backup and recovery strategies. Our automated daily backups with point-in-time recovery capabilities allow us to restore your data to any specific moment, minimizing data loss in case of incidents. Geographically distributed backup storage ensures your data is replicated across multiple locations, protecting against regional disasters and infrastructure failures. We perform regular backup integrity testing and validation to ensure all backup data is complete, accurate, and recoverable. Our disaster recovery procedures include defined Recovery Time Objectives (RTO) and Recovery Point Objectives (RPO) to ensure predictable and reliable recovery times. We maintain comprehensive business continuity planning and conduct regular testing to ensure our recovery procedures work effectively when needed.

10. Security Training and Awareness

Our team is trained in security best practices to ensure every member understands their role in protecting your data. We provide regular security awareness training for all employees, keeping them informed about the latest security threats and best practices. Our phishing simulation and social engineering training programs help team members recognize and respond appropriately to common attack vectors. We maintain secure coding practices and code review processes to ensure all software is developed with security in mind from the initial design phase. Incident response training and tabletop exercises prepare our team to handle security incidents effectively and efficiently. We provide continuous security education and updates to ensure our team remains current with evolving security threats and mitigation strategies.

11. Vulnerability Management

We proactively identify and address security vulnerabilities to maintain the highest level of protection for your data. Our regular vulnerability scanning and assessment processes continuously monitor our systems for potential security weaknesses, allowing us to identify and address issues before they can be exploited. Automated dependency vulnerability monitoring ensures that all third-party components and libraries are regularly checked for known security vulnerabilities. We maintain a comprehensive security patch management and deployment process to ensure all security updates are applied promptly and safely. Our bug bounty program encourages external security researchers to identify and report potential vulnerabilities, providing an additional layer of security oversight. We conduct regular security architecture reviews to ensure our overall security design remains robust and effective against evolving threats.

12. Data Privacy and Protection

We respect your privacy and protect your personal information through comprehensive privacy controls and procedures. Our data minimization principles ensure that we only collect and process the minimum amount of data necessary for providing our services. Purpose limitation and data retention policies ensure that your data is only used for its intended purpose and is not retained longer than necessary. We maintain robust user consent management and withdrawal mechanisms, giving you full control over how your data is used and allowing you to withdraw consent at any time. Our data subject rights fulfillment procedures ensure that you can easily exercise your rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data. We conduct privacy impact assessments for new features to identify and address potential privacy risks before implementation.

13. Security Transparency

We believe in transparency regarding our security practices and maintain open communication with our users about security matters. We provide regular security updates and communications to keep you informed about our security improvements and any relevant security developments. Our security incident disclosure procedures ensure that you are promptly notified of any security incidents that may affect your data, along with details about our response and mitigation efforts. We publish annual security reports and transparency documentation to provide comprehensive information about our security practices and performance. We maintain open communication channels for security concerns, allowing you to report potential issues and receive timely responses. Our security FAQ and best practices guidance help you understand our security measures and how to protect your own data effectively.

14. Your Security Responsibilities

While we implement robust security measures to protect your data, you also play an important role in maintaining the security of your account and information. We strongly recommend using strong, unique passwords for your account to prevent unauthorized access through credential-based attacks. Enabling multi-factor authentication when available adds an additional layer of protection to your account, significantly reducing the risk of unauthorized access. Keeping your devices and browsers updated ensures that you have the latest security patches and protection against known vulnerabilities. Being cautious of phishing attempts and suspicious links helps prevent social engineering attacks that could compromise your account. We encourage you to report any security concerns immediately so we can investigate and address potential issues promptly.

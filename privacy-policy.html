Privacy Policy

At DolzeAI Technologies Pvt Ltd, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our website, application, and related services (the "Services"). By using Dolze, you agree to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Who We Are

DolzeAI Technologies Pvt Ltd is headquartered at HSR, Bangalore, India. If you have questions about your data or this policy, you can contact us at support@dolze.ai.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information You Provide
Your name, email address, company name, and billing details.
Login credentials and account setup information.
Content you input into Dolze, such as brand descriptions and goals.

b. Automatically Collected Information
Device information, including browser type, operating system, and IP address.
Usage data, such as pages visited and features used.
Cookies and similar tracking technologies.

c. Payment Information
We utilize third-party payment processors like Stripe and do not store your credit card information on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
Enhance and improve the Dolze platform.
Personalize your experience with AI-driven recommendations.
Respond to your inquiries and support requests.
Process payments and manage subscriptions.
Send product updates, announcements, and marketing communications (you can opt out at any time).

4. AI & User Content

Dolze employs AI to generate content based on the information you provide. We do not utilize your private data to train third-party AI models. Content generated is exclusive to your account unless you decide to share it.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and analytics tools, such as Google Analytics, to understand how users interact with Dolze and to enhance performance. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Sharing Your Information

We never sell your data. We may share it only with:
Service providers, such as cloud hosting and payment processors.
Legal authorities if required by law.
Business transfers in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale.
All partners are contractually obligated to protect your data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

8. Security

We implement best-in-class security measures, including encryption, access controls, and secure data storage. However, no system is 100% secure, so we encourage you to use strong passwords and monitor your account activity.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, such as the India, EU, UK, or California, you may have rights to:
Access or correct your data.
Request deletion of your data.
Object to certain uses of your data.
Export your data (data portability).
To exercise these rights, email us at support@dolze.ai.

10. Children's Privacy

Dolze is not intended for children under 13 years old (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. International Users

If you are located outside India, please be aware that your data may be processed in India or other jurisdictions. We comply with international privacy standards, including GDPR and CCPA where applicable.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we'll notify you via email or within the platform. Continued use of Dolze signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.
